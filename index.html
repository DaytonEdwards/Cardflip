The Basics


Rugby has many big rules to follow. The rule system is to make sure everyone has a fair chance and as safe as possible. Some of the rules are the following: rules are to not tackle people with having ball in hand, dont go around ruck formation, pass only side or behind you, and lastly to make sure no to knock the ball infront of you.
