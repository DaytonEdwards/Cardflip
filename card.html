The Rule Cards

Dont tackle a nonball holder

-tackling someone without the ball will be a penality


-this is due to possible injurys and "obstruction"

Rucking line

-The person with the ball down has a line to his to toes


-You can't run around that imaginary line, you must break through it

Passing

-The person with the ball can only pass behind or to their side


-Throwing the ball infront at any angle will cause a penalty

Knocking

-A knock is when a person receives the ball but drops infront of them


-This action will lead to an automatic ball to the other team

Gear

-The only gear allowed is a soft headmat and smaller/thin shoulder pad


-This makes tackling and being hit more involving

Obstruction

-People can not be in the way of the ball/ player with ball


-This isn't basketball and you will end up with a penality

One second rule

-The person who is tackled must let go of the ball within 1 second


-purposfully fumble and hurry to put the ball to your or you will get a penality

Respect the Reff

-1 captain for both teams and only that player is able to speak to the reff


-A penality will be given if not followed
